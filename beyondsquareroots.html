Research and Explore

Now that you have learned about nth roots, research a real-world situation where roots are applied. Here are some ideas:

  • Physics: Investigate how nth roots are used to calculate physical properties like volume or energy.
  • Mathematics: Explore how roots are used in solving equations involving polynomials.
  • Finance: Understand how roots are used in calculating compound interest rates.

Write down your calculation and explain how you used nth roots. Be ready to share your findings with the class and discuss why these calculations are useful.
