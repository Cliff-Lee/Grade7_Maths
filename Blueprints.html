Grade 7 Geometry Unit: Exploring Angles and Figures

Introduction to the Blueprints Unit

This unit focuses on the key concept of Form, connecting mathematics to real-world urban planning. Through the concepts of Generalization and Space, students will explore how geometry is applied in designing cities and structures. Our global context is Globalization and Sustainability with an emphasis on urban planning. The central idea is that urban planning relies on understanding how to generalize spatial forms. Students will build critical and creative thinking skills by analyzing and solving spatial problems related to real-world urban environments.
