MYP Mathematics: Investigating Patterns

Practice Assessment: Exploring Patterns in Fractions

Consider the following sequence of fractions:

1/7, 2/7, 3/7, ...

Task Instructions

You are to investigate the patterns within the given sequence of fractions. Complete the following tasks:

  1. Identify and describe any patterns you observe in the sequence of fractions.
  2. Convert each fraction to its decimal equivalent. Analyze any patterns you notice in these decimal forms.
  3. Explain why 4/7 is included in the sequence and how it fits into the observed pattern.
  4. Predict the next two fractions in the sequence. Justify your predictions based on the patterns you've identified.
  5. Generalize the pattern by formulating a rule or expression that represents the sequence. Use mathematical notation to express your generalization.

Hint: Rotate the circle to observe the pattern of repeating decimals.
